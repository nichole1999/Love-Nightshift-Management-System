üéØ Spherical Scaling System Framework

The key to unlocking new angles and scaling Facebook ads profitably. This system allows us to reach different sectors of the algorithm by being specific with our messaging.

Step 1: Problem + Avatar = Angle

Formula: Specific Problem + Target Persona = New Angle

  • Don't use broad terms like "acne problem"
  • Be specific: "hormonal acne," "cystic acne," "mask acne"
  • Pair with specific avatar: "teenager," "adult woman," "gym-goer"
  • Example: "Adult woman with hormonal acne"

Step 2: Market Awareness Level

Target: Match your message to awareness stage

  • Unaware: "Do you have weird spots on your skin?"
  • Problem Aware: "Struggling with sleep issues?"
  • Solution Aware: "Tried sleep masks before?"
  • Product Aware: "Compare our sleep mask..."
  • Most Aware: "20% off your sleep solution"

Step 3: Hooks & Format Variations

Scale: Create multiple variations per angle

  • Visual hooks (different demographics/people)
  • Verbal hooks (different messaging/tone)
  • Text hooks (different copy approaches)
  • Format variations (UGC, VSL, podcast style)

Hook Testing Strategy:

  • Vary between persona-specific and general

  • Test different demographic faces per hook

  • Make it specific to target audience
  • Use language customers actually use
  • Create curiosity gap
  • Include product keywords for clarity

Editor Guidelines & Expectations

Video Editing Standards:

  • Market: Older audience (not fast-paced kids content)
  • Style: Clear, premium, luxury, minimalist, authentic
  • Cuts: 3-4 seconds per cut, not too quick
  • Music: No music for educational/doctor videos
  • Captions: Native organic style, easy to read
  • Safe Zones: Must fit within platform requirements

‚ùå What NOT to Do:

  • Fast-paced, distracting transitions
  • Loud, in-your-face editing
  • Cheap-looking B-roll footage
  • Gimmicky or ugly effects
  • Change footage AND captions simultaneously
  • Cover important parts with captions
  • Use copyrighted music
